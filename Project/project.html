Routines

LEGS

Leg exercises help to tone the legs and increase muscle strength. The legs are a fundamental part of the body both in daily life and in sports practice. So it is very important not to leave them aside and work them to improve health and training performance.

  1. Squat: 3 sets x 6 reps.
  2. Romanian Deadlift: 3 sets x 8 reps.
  3. Bulgarian Split Squat: 3 sets x 10 reps.
  4. Seated Leg Curl: 3 sets x 12 reps.
  5. Leg Extension: 2 sets x 12 reps.
  6. Standing Calf Raise: 3 sets x 15 reps.

CHEST

How do you get bigger, toned, and toned pecs? That is the question asked by some of those who decide to start training in the gym and whose goal is to achieve an increase in muscle mass. To achieve this goal, three fundamental strategies must be taken into account. The first of these has to do with a volume routine to gain muscle mass, through a series of exercises to achieve the perfect chest. It is also essential to carry out a basic diet to enlarge muscle, in which a high protein intake will be essential. And, of course, rest, overtraining must be avoided by all means. Adequate relaxation times are essential for the muscles to recover and thus be able to develop.

  1. Bench press with barbell
  2. Opening with dumbbells
  3. Inclined barbell press
  4. Archer's flexion
  5. Pull-over with dumbbells

ARM

Arm strength exercises will allow you to increase the power of the upper limbs by developing their muscle groups. The most effective are those that are performed with resistance, either with dumbbells or with another instrument.

  1. Triceps push-ups on a chair.
  2. Wall triceps extension.
  3. Shoulder press with dumbbells.
  4. Diamond push-ups.
