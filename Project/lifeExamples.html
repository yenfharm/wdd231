Life Examples

Arnold Schwarzenegger

Do something every day. Don't sit on the couch and eat chips and do nothing. Go out and train. You feel great when you work out. It's great when you feel strong and also you can do a lot of sports to stay healthy. Everybody has 24 hours in a day. You have to decide how you want to go through life. I simply ask you to be better today than you were yesterday. It's simple, and it's the key to a healthier future.

Chris Hemsworth

It's about prioritizing what's important. I have an incredibly busy life, but with just 20 or 30 minutes a day to train, I can already see the benefits. If I don't find those 20 or 30 minutes, then the rest of the day becomes uphill. I recommend people make an effort to find that time to work out because once you feel the benefits, it's hard to ignore them. I think it's relatively easy to find time in your schedule for those 20 or 30 minutes.

Henry Cavill

It is not necessary to lift a lot of weight every day to achieve results. I have learned that the weight is not the most important thing, but the training itself. If you ever feel embarrassed in the gym because there's always someone next to you lifting heavy weights, it's not worth it. Complete your routine and make every workout count. You may end up looking better than whoever is next to you with heavy weights.
