Training Food

Important nutrients before training

Mostly carbohydrates or carbohydrates will have to be ingested. It must be taken into account that there are different types of carbohydrates: those of fast absorption and those that have a slower absorption. Therefore, it is important to watch the timing of the pre-training intake. A slow-absorbing carbohydrate half an hour before training will not help us, as our body will not have been able to assimilate it yet. It is recommended to eat 2-3 hours before. If for any reason it is necessary to eat 30 min-1h before, it will be necessary to use fast absorption carbohydrates.

  1. White bread toast with quince jelly, jam or honey, adding a little fresh cheese.
  2. Dates or hazelnuts.
  3. Fruits such as banana, which is simple and practical.
  4. Bags of dehydrated fruit, such as mangoes.
  5. AYogurt with cereals and fruit.
  6. Oat and banana cake.

Important nutrients after training

Once the training is finished, the important moment of recovery arrives. Protein and carbohydrate intake is mainly recommended.

  1. Snacks such as fresh cheese or fruit smoothies.
  2. Omelet or egg and toast, if we add a fruit it would be a very complete post-workout breakfast.
  3. If it is close to lunch or dinner time, we could make oily fish, vegetables and baked moniato
  4. Or, a salad with rice, salmon, avocado, tomato, lettuce, corn, carrots... choose the one that pleases you the most!

Anti-inflammatories and antioxidants can also be added. Remember the importance of rehydration.
