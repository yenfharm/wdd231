Project Site Plan

Site Name:

Sports Shopping

Site Purpose:

This is a site for shopping and also about sports.

Color Schema:

  • Baby Powder #F9F7F3: Page Background
  • Cocoa Brown #C97548: Page headings

Target Audience Scenarios:

Scenario Question 1:
Scenario: Maya, a 28-year-old freelance graphic designer, needs to create a professional online portfolio to showcase her work and attract new clients.
User Goal: Maya wants to easily upload and organize her design projects, and create a visually appealing portfolio that effectively communicates her skills and style.
Pain Points: Maya is not tech-savvy and has limited time to learn new tools. She's frustrated with existing portfolio platforms that are cluttered and difficult to use.
Design Requirements:
Intuitive upload and organization system for design projects Customizable templates and layouts for a unique visual identity Easy integration with social media and other online platforms


Scenario Question 2:
Scenario: Juan, a 35-year-old father of two, wants to help his children develop healthy eating habits and cook meals together as a family.
User Goal: Juan wants to find simple, kid-friendly recipes and meal planning tools that make cooking fun and educational for his children.
Pain Points: Juan is busy with work and family responsibilities, and struggles to find time to plan and prepare healthy meals. He's overwhelmed by the number of recipe websites and apps available.
Design Requirements:
Simple, easy-to-follow recipe instructions and videos Meal planning tools that allow for customization and grocery lists Interactive features that engage children in the cooking process, such as games and quizzes
